WowWords Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

Summary: WowWords is designed with privacy as a core principle. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

1. Introduction

Welcome to WowWords, an educational vocabulary and essay writing app designed for students. NeuralAutomate ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of children who use our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use WowWords.

2. Information We Collect

We collect NO personal information. WowWords operates entirely on your device without sending any data to external servers. Here's what is stored locally on your device:

  • Student Profile: Name and avatar selection (stored locally using Hive database)
  • Learning Progress: Vocabulary mastery scores, essay drafts, and achievement records
  • Usage Statistics: Daily streak counts and practice history
  • AI Model Data: On-device language model for vocabulary evaluation (runs locally, no data transmitted)

All this information remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

3. How We Use Information

Since all data is stored locally, it is used solely to:

  • Personalize your learning experience with your name and avatar
  • Track your vocabulary mastery and essay writing progress
  • Maintain your daily streak and achievement records
  • Provide AI-powered vocabulary evaluation (processed entirely on-device)

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using secure, encrypted storage mechanisms. Because data never leaves your device:

  • There are no external servers that could be breached
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • Your information cannot be accessed by us or any third party

5. Third-Party Services

WowWords does NOT use any third-party services that collect data. Specifically:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services
  • No external AI APIs - all AI processing happens on your device

6. Children's Privacy

WowWords is designed for students in grades 2-8. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not collect any personal information from children
  • No account creation or email required
  • No data is transmitted externally
  • Parents can delete all app data by removing the app from the device
  • Our app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) by not collecting any personal information

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app itself
  • Deletion: Delete the app to remove all stored data permanently
  • Control: You have complete control over your data at all times

8. Data Sharing

We do NOT share any information because we do NOT collect any information. There is no data sharing with:

  • Advertisers
  • Analytics companies
  • Social networks
  • Any third parties

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes.
