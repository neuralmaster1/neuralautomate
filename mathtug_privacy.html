MathTug Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Neural Automate built the MathTug app as a free-to-play educational math game. This Privacy Policy describes how MathTug handles information.

Information We Collect

MathTug collects the following information when you create an account and use the app:

  • User ID (username): Used to identify your account and enable multiplayer matchmaking.
  • Gameplay data: Scores, win/loss records, and other usage statistics used to track progress and rank players.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Providing app functionality, including account login and gameplay.
  • Displaying leaderboards and player statistics.
  • Maintaining fair play and ranking players.

Data Storage

Your information is stored on secure servers operated by Neural Automate. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Children's Privacy

MathTug does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.
