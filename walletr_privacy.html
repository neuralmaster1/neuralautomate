Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 11, 2025

Overview

Walletr is a non-custodial Bitcoin wallet application for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Walletr does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any identifying information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not have access to your Bitcoin or recovery phrase
  • We do not store any data on our servers

Local Data Storage

All wallet data is stored locally on your device:

  • Recovery Phrase: Your 12-word recovery phrase is encrypted and stored using iOS Keychain
  • Bitcoin Addresses: Generated locally on your device
  • Transaction History: Stored locally on your device
  • Private Keys: Derived on-demand and never persisted

No wallet data is transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Walletr uses the following third-party services to function properly:

Blockstream API (blockstream.info)

  • Used for: Checking Bitcoin balances and broadcasting transactions
  • Data shared: Bitcoin addresses and transaction data
  • Privacy Policy: blockstream.com/privacy

Mempool.space API (mempool.space)

  • Used for: Network fee estimation
  • Data shared: API requests for fee data
  • Privacy Policy: mempool.space/about

CoinGecko API (coingecko.com)

  • Used for: Bitcoin price data in USD
  • Data shared: API requests for price data
  • Privacy Policy: coingecko.com/en/privacy

Note: These services may collect IP addresses and request data according to their own privacy policies. We do not control these services or their data practices.

Security

We take security seriously:

  • Your recovery phrase is encrypted using iOS Keychain (AES-256 encryption)
  • Private keys are derived on-demand and never stored permanently
  • All cryptographic operations happen locally on your device
  • We use industry-standard Bitcoin protocols (BIP39, BIP32, BIP84, BIP143)
  • The app code is obfuscated to prevent reverse engineering

Your Responsibilities

Important: Walletr is a non-custodial wallet.

This means:

  • You are solely responsible for your recovery phrase
  • You must backup your recovery phrase securely offline
  • Lost recovery phrases cannot be recovered by us or anyone else
  • You are responsible for the security of your device
  • You are responsible for all transactions made with your wallet
  • We cannot reverse transactions or recover lost funds

Children's Privacy

Walletr is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
