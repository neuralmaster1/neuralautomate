EchoPath – Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-08-07

EchoPath is provided by NeuralAutomate (“we”, “us”). This policy explains what information the app processes and how it is handled.

What the app processes

EchoPath monitors your device’s call state and network type to help you understand when calls occur over Wi‑Fi Calling (VoWiFi) or cellular. The app processes the following on your device only:

  • Call events: start/end timestamps, a random call identifier, and call duration.
  • Network transitions during a call (e.g., Wi‑Fi/VoWiFi, LTE, 5G).
  • App settings you choose.

EchoPath does not access or record call audio, phone numbers, contacts, messages, photos, precise location, advertising identifiers, or any content of your communications.

How data is used

Data is used solely to show history and analytics inside the app. We do not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Storage and retention

All data is stored locally on your device (SQLite). You can delete it at any time from the app’s settings (Clear Logs) or by uninstalling the app.

Permissions

  • Call/telephony status (CallKit): to detect when calls start/end.
  • Network information (Network framework): to determine current connection type (Wi‑Fi/VoWiFi or cellular).

The app does not request microphone, camera, contacts, photos, or location permissions.

Analytics, tracking, and ads

EchoPath contains no third‑party analytics SDKs, ad SDKs, or tracking. We do not track you across apps or websites.

Security

Because data remains on your device, it is protected by your device’s security features. We recommend keeping your OS updated.

Children’s privacy

EchoPath is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will post updates on this page with a new effective date.
