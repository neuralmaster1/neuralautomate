Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 23, 2025
Last Updated: September 23, 2025

Habitr ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Habitr mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Habit Data: Names, descriptions, categories, and completion status of your habits
  • Settings and Preferences: App settings, notification preferences, theme choices
  • Feedback: When you contact us for support or provide feedback

Automatically Collected Information

  • App Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app, crash reports
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Local Storage: All habit data is stored locally on your device by default

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number) unless you provide it for support
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect sensitive personal information

2. How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Core Functionality: To provide habit tracking, analytics, and reminder features
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the app experience
  • Customer Support: To respond to your questions and provide assistance
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Local Data Processing

  • Most of your data is processed and stored locally on your device
  • Habit data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync (Premium feature)
  • You maintain full control over your personal habit information

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

  • Purpose: To manage in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Data Collected: Purchase transactions, subscription status, device identifiers
  • Privacy Policy: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Apple App Store

  • Purpose: App distribution and purchase processing
  • Data Collected: Purchase information, device identifiers for fraud prevention
  • Privacy Policy: Apple Privacy Policy

Local Notifications

  • Purpose: To send habit reminders on your device
  • Data: Notification content is generated locally and never transmitted
  • Control: You can disable notifications in device settings

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Limited Sharing Circumstances

We may share information only in these situations:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with legal obligations, court orders, or government requests
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in app operation (like RevenueCat for subscriptions)
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition (users would be notified)

Data Protection

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
  • Third-party services are carefully vetted for privacy and security practices

5. Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

  • View Your Data: All habit data is accessible within the app
  • Export Data: Premium users can export their habit data
  • Delete Data: You can delete individual habits or all data within the app
  • Account Deletion: Contact us to delete any account-related information

Communication Preferences

  • Notifications: Control habit reminders through device and app settings
  • Marketing: We do not send promotional communications
  • Support: You can contact us at any time regarding your data

Regional Rights

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights such as:

  • Right to access your data (GDPR - European Union)
  • Right to data portability (GDPR - European Union)
  • Right to deletion (CCPA - California, GDPR - European Union)
  • Right to correct inaccurate data

6. Data Retention

Local Data

  • Habit data is stored on your device until you delete it
  • App settings are retained until you uninstall the app
  • You have full control over local data retention

Cloud Data (Premium Feature)

  • Synced data is retained as long as you maintain an active subscription
  • Data is deleted within 30 days after subscription cancellation
  • You can request immediate deletion by contacting support

Analytics Data

  • Anonymous usage analytics are retained for up to 2 years
  • This data cannot be linked back to individual users
  • Used solely for app improvement purposes

7. Children's Privacy

  • Habitr is not directed to children under 13 years of age
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately
  • Parents who believe their child has provided information should contact us

8. International Data Transfers

  • Your data is primarily stored locally on your device
  • Cloud sync data (if enabled) may be stored on servers in various countries
  • We ensure appropriate safeguards for international data transfers
  • All transfers comply with applicable privacy laws

9. Data Security

Technical Safeguards

  • Industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Secure coding practices and vulnerability testing
  • Limited access to systems containing user data

Your Role in Security

  • Keep your device secure with passwords/biometric locks
  • Keep the app updated to the latest version
  • Report any security concerns to us immediately

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements
  • Material changes will be communicated through the app or email if provided
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy
  • Previous versions will be archived and available upon request

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@neuralautomate.xyz
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours
  • App Feedback: Use the feedback feature in Settings

For privacy-specific concerns, please include "Privacy" in your subject line.

12. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your information includes:

  • Consent: For optional features like analytics
  • Contract Performance: For providing the app service and subscription management
  • Legitimate Interest: For app improvement and security
  • Legal Obligation: For compliance with applicable laws

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)
  • Access personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights
