Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2, 2025

Our Commitment: Naturalr is built with privacy at its core. Your health data stays on your device and is never uploaded to our servers. We believe your menstrual health information is deeply personal and should remain under your control.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

Naturalr stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Menstrual cycle dates and predictions
  • Symptoms, moods, and notes you choose to log
  • App preferences and settings
  • Notification preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

1.2 Subscription Information

If you purchase a premium subscription, payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive only:

  • Confirmation of your subscription status (active/expired)
  • Subscription type (monthly/yearly)
  • Anonymous transaction identifiers

We do not receive your payment details, credit card information, or Apple ID.

2. How We Use Information

Your locally stored health data is used solely to:

  • Provide cycle predictions and insights within the app
  • Send you optional local notifications and reminders
  • Generate exports when you request them

Subscription status information is used only to unlock premium features.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health data with anyone.

Your cycle and health information remains exclusively on your device. We have no access to it.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is stored locally using secure device storage
  • Optional app lock feature with biometric authentication
  • No cloud synchronization means no server-side vulnerabilities
  • Data exports are generated locally and controlled by you

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it. You can:

  • Delete individual entries at any time within the app
  • Clear all data through the app settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all stored data

6. Third-Party Services

Naturalr uses the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. RevenueCat processes subscription status only and does not have access to your health data. See their privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing and app distribution

7. Children's Privacy

Naturalr is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (it's stored locally on your device)
  • Export your data in standard formats
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Use the app without creating an account

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
